## 2025-05-22 - Login Page Micro-UX Improvements
**Learning:** Even without full CSS support in the development environment, standard HTML accessibility features like `htmlFor` and `id` linking are crucial for screen readers. Providing one-click demo fills significantly reduces friction for first-time users and during development.
**Action:** Always link labels to inputs and provide interactive demo account helpers in authentication flows to enhance both accessibility and developer/user experience.
