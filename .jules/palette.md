# Palette Journal - Critical UX/Accessibility Learnings

This journal documents critical UX and accessibility learnings for the BuildTrack AI application.

Format: `## YYYY-MM-DD - [Title] **Learning:** [UX/a11y insight] **Action:** [How to apply next time]`

## 2026-04-20 - Enhanced Login UX
**Learning:** High-impact micro-enhancements for `Login.tsx` include semantic `htmlFor`/`id` linking for accessibility, implementing a `showPassword` toggle with Eye/EyeOff icons, and adding role-specific 'Quick Login' buttons. 'Quick Login' buttons significantly streamline testing and demoing by removing friction for repeat users.
**Action:** Always prioritize accessible form labeling and consider 'Quick Login' or 'Demo' buttons for construction/enterprise apps where multiple personas are tested frequently.
