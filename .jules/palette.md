## 2025-05-15 - Login Page Micro-UX Improvements
**Learning:** High-impact micro-enhancements for `Login.tsx` include semantic `htmlFor`/`id` linking for accessibility, implementing a `showPassword` toggle with Eye/EyeOff icons, and adding role-specific 'Quick Login' buttons for convenience. Using `type="button"` for auxiliary buttons within or near forms is critical to prevent accidental submissions.
**Action:** Always include password visibility toggles and quick-login shortcuts for demo accounts. Ensure all form fields have proper label associations and focus-visible indicators.
